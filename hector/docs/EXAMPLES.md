## Response Format Examples

### Summary Format
> **Query**: What is Section 302 BNS?
>
> **Answer**: Section 302 of BNS deals with murder. Whoever commits murder shall be punished with death or imprisonment for life, and shall also be liable to fine.
>
> **Source**: Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita 2023, Section 302

### Detailed Format
> **Query**: What is Section 302 BNS?
>
> **Answer**: [Full analysis with chapter context, related sections, case precedents]
>
> **Related Provisions**: Sections 303, 304, 307 BNS

### Citations Format
> **Query**: What is Section 302 BNS?
>
> **Source**: BNS Section 302
> **Chapter**: Chapter III - Offences Against Human Body
> **Punishment**: Death or life imprisonment + fine
> **Related**: IPC Section 302 (corresponding)
